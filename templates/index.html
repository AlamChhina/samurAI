Frequently Asked Questions

What video formats do you support?

We support all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and more. We also support YouTube URLs and other video platforms.

How accurate is the transcription?

Our AI uses OpenAI's Whisper model, which provides industry-leading accuracy for speech recognition across multiple languages and accents.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll continue to have Pro access until the end of your billing period.

What happens to my data?

Free users' data is automatically deleted after 7 days. Pro users' data is kept for 30 days. You can download your files anytime before deletion.
